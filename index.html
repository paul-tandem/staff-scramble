What is the point of this game?

Staff Scramble is a lighthearted look at a serious risk: Key Person Dependency. In the game, your "character" represents a vital employee. The hazards they face—flu season, sudden resignations, or even a simple vacation—are real-world events that can halt your business if your processes aren't institutionalized.

What is "Key Person Dependency"?

It occurs when a business relies too heavily on the specific knowledge, skills, or presence of one or two individuals. If that person isn't available, the business can't function. Our goal is to help you move from a people-dependent business to a process-driven one.

Why are these "hazards" so dangerous?

In a perfect world, your business runs like a machine. In the real world, people get sick, burnt out, or move on to new opportunities. If your SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) and automations live only in an employee's head, your business is effectively "scrambling" every single day.

How do I stop the scramble?

By building systems that outlast any individual contributor. This involves:

  • Documenting your core organization processes.
  • Automating repetitive tasks to reduce manual errors.
  • Cross-training team members so there is no "single point of failure."
